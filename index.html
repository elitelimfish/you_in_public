Delete Your Account and Data

If you wish to delete your account and associated personal data from You In?, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Email us at YouInFeedback@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your registered email address or user ID
  • Confirmation that you want all associated data deleted

What Data is Deleted

  • Account details (email, username, profile info)
  • App usage data (preferences, settings)
  • Any stored personal information
  • Event participation history
  • User-generated content

Data Retention Policy

We permanently delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request. Some data may be retained temporarily to comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please email us at YouInFeedback@gmail.com.
