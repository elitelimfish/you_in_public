Child Safety Standards - You In?

Child Safety Contact: YouInFeedback@gmail.com

Last Updated: January 2024

Zero Tolerance Policy

"You In?" maintains a zero-tolerance policy regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and take proactive measures to prevent, detect, and report any form of child exploitation or abuse.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on our platform:

  • Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or attempts to sexually exploit minors
  • Content that sexualizes or endangers children
  • Sharing or soliciting images or information about minors
  • Any attempt to traffic or exploit children
  • Creating accounts with the intention of targeting minors

Reporting Mechanisms

How to Report Concerns:

  1. Use the in-app "Report" button located on any user profile or event
  2. Email our dedicated safety team at YouInFeedback@gmail.com
  3. Include "URGENT - Safety Concern" in the subject line for immediate attention

Our Commitment to Safety

  • Swift Action: We investigate all reports within 24 hours
  • Law Enforcement: We cooperate with authorities and report illegal content
  • Account Termination: Immediate permanent ban for any CSAE violations
  • Content Removal: Immediate removal of any reported CSAM

Prevention Measures

  • Automated detection systems for suspicious behavior
  • Regular safety audits and updates
  • Staff training on identifying and handling CSAE concerns
  • Age verification processes
  • Restricted messaging features for new accounts

Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable laws regarding child safety and reporting requirements, including:

  • Mandatory reporting to relevant authorities
  • Preservation of evidence for law enforcement
  • Cooperation with investigations
  • Regular policy updates to reflect current legislation
