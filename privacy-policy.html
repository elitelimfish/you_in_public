Privacy Policy for "You In?"

Effective Date: January 2024

Contact Email: YouInFeedback@gmail.com

1. Introduction

"You In?" ("we", "our", or "us") is a simple social app that allows users to add friends and invite them to events. We take user privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Name or username provided by you during signup or use
  • Email address or phone number (based on login method)
  • Friend connections (who you invite or connect with)

b. Event Information

Details of events you create, join, or invite others to.

c. Device Information

Basic technical info like device type and app version (used to improve performance).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable friend connections and event invites
  • To display your events and history
  • To debug issues and improve app performance
  • To respond to support inquiries

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use anonymized, aggregate data for analytics or improvement.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only while your account is active or as needed to provide the app. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures. However, no system is 100% secure, and by using the app, you acknowledge this risk.

7. Children's Privacy

"You In?" is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we do, we delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights and Account Deletion

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To delete your account, email us at YouInFeedback@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account". We will complete the deletion within 30 days.

For detailed account deletion instructions, please visit our Account Deletion page.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Effective Date".

10. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at YouInFeedback@gmail.com.
